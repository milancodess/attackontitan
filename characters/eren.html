Eren Jaeger

Full Name: Eren Jaeger

Birthdate: March 30th

Age:

  • At the start of the series: 10 (at the beginning of Attack on Titan)
  • Post time-skip: Approximately 19 when he died (in the The Dawn of Humanity)

Appearance: Eren is depicted as a young man with short, dark brown hair and sharp green eyes. He typically wears the standard uniform of the Scout Regiment, consisting of a brown leather jacket, white shirt, and green cloak.

Background: Eren grew up in the Shiganshina District with his parents, Grisha and Carla Jaeger. His childhood was marked by the trauma of witnessing his mother's death at the hands of a Titan, which fueled his intense hatred towards them. After joining the military, Eren becomes determined to eradicate the Titans and uncover the truth about their origins.

Personality: Initially depicted as hot-headed and impulsive, Eren is driven by a strong sense of justice and a desire for revenge against the Titans. As the series progresses, his character matures, becoming more strategic and calculating while still retaining his fierce determination.

Abilities:

  • Titan Shifter: Eren possesses the ability to transform into a Titan, initially manifesting as the Attack Titan. He later gains the powers of the Founding Titan and the War Hammer Titan.
  • Combat Skills: Eren is skilled in hand-to-hand combat and is proficient in using the Vertical Maneuvering Equipment, allowing him to navigate and fight effectively in Titan-infested environments.
  • Intellect: While not initially portrayed as academically inclined, Eren demonstrates keen strategic thinking and leadership qualities as the series progresses.

Relationships

  • Family:Eren's parents are Grisha and Carla Jaeger. He also develops close bonds with his adoptive sister, Mikasa Ackerman, and his childhood friend, Armin Arlert.
  • Love Interest: While it's not explicitly stated in the series, Eren's relationship with Mikasa Ackerman is portrayed with romantic undertones, though the extent of his feelings towards her is ambiguous.

Role in the Story:Eren serves as the main protagonist of Attack on Titan, driving much of the narrative with his quest for vengeance and later, his pursuit of freedom and understanding. His journey is central to exploring themes of war, power, and the complexity of human nature within the series.
